Stress 101

One of the most stressful things about stress is when you can't get rid of it.

Honestly, it's almost as stressful as the stress itself.

I made this website because I've found that the best way to get through stress is to pinpoint what's causing it.

I do it through writing. The act of just writing out my worries is like a weight off my chest.

I hope it's that way for you too. Try out Stressbox and email me what you think!
